CHAPTER 04
The approach

We didn't replace your team. We removed the friction before them.

Most AI automation projects try to replace human responses. That produces robotic output and erodes customer trust. We took the opposite angle: keep humans writing replies, but eliminate the 80% of cognitive load that sits before the reply.

Classification first. Never generation.

The system classifies every incoming email across 6 dimensions (urgency, intent, product area, account tier, needs-reply, complexity), drafts a preliminary response for routine cases, and routes the ticket to the exact right person — all within 45 seconds of arrival.

Humans still own the final reply. But they start from a pre-analyzed, pre-drafted, pre-routed ticket instead of a blank inbox.

01

Classify with precision

Claude Sonnet 4.6 tags emails across 6 dimensions. 94% accuracy on domain after 3 prompt iterations.

02

Draft, don't decide

For routine tickets, Claude pre-writes a response using the team's tone-of-voice corpus. Humans edit 20% and send.

03

Route with context

Tickets land in the assigned rep's Slack with summary tags, customer history, and draft reply — ready to act on.

04

Escalate intelligently

Urgent + enterprise tier → instant SMS to on-call lead. Low-complexity + free tier → queued for batch.
