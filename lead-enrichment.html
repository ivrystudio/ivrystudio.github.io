The problem

Their reps were drowning in leads —
and missing the ones that mattered.

A Series B B2B SaaS had just scaled their outbound machine. Ads, cold email, LinkedIn, content — leads were pouring in from 11 different sources at roughly 240 inbound contacts per week.

Their 4-person SDR team was spending 3 minutes per lead just enriching before they could even decide whether to reach out. 240 leads × 3 min = 12 hours per week of pure manual lookup work. Across 4 reps, that was 48 hours gone. One full-time role, burned on copy-paste.

Worse: by the time a qualified lead got touched, 17 hours had passed on average. Competitors were closing the ones they wanted most.

02 / 07
The approach

We treated enrichment as a
deterministic pipeline, not a chatbot.

Most "AI sales tools" are glorified chat wrappers. We went the other direction: a deterministic, auditable pipeline where every step has a clear input, a measurable output, and a cost in dollars. Sales ops teams can audit every single enrichment run.

01 / PRINCIPLE
Data first, LLM second
Structured data sources (Clearbit, Apollo, LinkedIn API) run before Claude ever sees the lead. The LLM synthesizes — it doesn't hallucinate facts.
02 / PRINCIPLE
Every output is traceable
Every enriched field has a source attribution. Every score has a reasoning log. Reps can click a cell and see exactly why.
03 / PRINCIPLE
Humans make the final call
The pipeline proposes routing, scoring, and icebreaker drafts. A rep approves in one click — or rejects with one keystroke. Learning loop built in.
04 / PRINCIPLE
Costs are visible, per lead
Every enrichment costs ~$0.08 in API fees. Dashboard shows exact spend per rep, per source, per day. No black boxes.

The process

21 days. Four milestones.
Every invoice tied to a deliverable.

DAY 1–3
Audit & scoping
Shadowed 3 SDRs for a full day each. Mapped the exact steps they took, where they stalled, what data they looked up first. No slide decks — just observation.
milestone 1 20% · $840
DAY 4–10
Core pipeline build
Built the 10-source enrichment engine on n8n. Claude Sonnet 4.5 prompts locked after 3 iterations. First run on live leads at day 8.
milestone 2 35% · $1,470

CRM + Slack integration
Deep HubSpot integration with dedup logic. Slack cards with one-click accept/reject. Feedback loop wired back into the scoring model.
